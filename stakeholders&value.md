# Stakeholders & Value

## Stakeholders (Target Audience)

Marketing managers: Main beneficiary of the project, as managers get a guidance for timing and placement of advertisements in peak purchasing times

Sales and revenue teams: Can use the patterns recognised by the project to forecast demand sales and promotions

Game publishers: Get to understand long term gaming patterns and player behavior 

Data engineers, architects: Responsible for maintaining the AWS infrastructure and ensuring data flow 

## Business Value

Optimized advertizing: The business can shift marketing budgets to when data shows higher engagement

Increased revenue: Targeting high-demand periods helps in increasing overall game sales

Customer base growth: Analyzing patterns allows for better-timed "free-to-play" weekends or discounts, helping to acquire new users

Data-driven decision making: structured pipeline that uses real-time and historical data for accuracy

Cost efficiency: Using a serverless architecture ensures that the company only pays for the data processed
